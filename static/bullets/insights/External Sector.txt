On 14 April, the COP traded at 4,424 per USD, appreciating 7.8% month
on month. The COP gained ground following rising oil prices and recent
rate hikes by the Central Bank. The COP is seen depreciating by end-
2023. Oil prices, the BanRep’s interest rate path relative to the Fed’s and
the president’s policy agenda pose risks. FocusEconomics analysts see
the peso ending 2023 at 4,703 per USD and 2024 at 4,565 per USD.
